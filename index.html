Our Story

Pobitra Ghee was born from a mother's love for pure, traditional food. Using the ancient bilona method passed down through generations, we hand-churn every batch with patience and care. Our ghee is made from the milk of grass-fed cows, locally sourced from trusted farmers who share our values.

What started as a mother's kitchen experiment has now become a mission — to bring the authentic taste of tradition to every home in India.
